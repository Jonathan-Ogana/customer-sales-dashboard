# Customer Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of customer sales performance across multiple countries, sales representatives, and time periods. The report is designed to help business stakeholders monitor key sales metrics, identify top-performing regions and representatives, and understand customer distribution patterns.

The dashboard transforms raw sales and customer data into actionable business insights through visual analytics and dynamic filtering capabilities.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyse customer purchasing behaviour.
- Identify top-performing sales representatives.
- Compare sales performance across countries.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Evaluate customer distribution geographically.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard provides high-level business metrics including:

- **Total Sales Revenue:** $4.08M
- **Average Order Amount:** $1.86K
- **Total Customers:** 269
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 7.61K

---

### Visualisations

#### 1. Sales Performance by Representative
- Compares sales generated by each sales representative.
- Highlights top and low-performing sales personnel.

#### 2. Sales Performance by Country
- Displays revenue contribution across different countries.
- Supports geographical performance comparison.

#### 3. Customer Distribution Map
- Visualises customer locations worldwide.
- Identifies areas with high customer concentration.

#### 4. Monthly Sales Trend
- Tracks sales performance across months.
- Reveals seasonality and sales fluctuations.

---

### Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter the report using:

- **Order Year**
  - 2013
  - 2014
  - 2015

- **City**
  - Multiple city-level selections available
